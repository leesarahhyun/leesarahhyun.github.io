LOGO DESIGN

1004 TAXI (TAXI SERVICE)

1004 TAXI is a taxi service mainly serving the KTOWN community. In Korean, the pronunciation for "1004" also means angel, which I incorporated into the logo. For the body of the logo, I used the destination symbol in order to convey the idea that 1004 TAXI is a service that gets you to your final destination.
