POSTER DESIGN

A WINTER WONDERLAND (personal project)

The festive month of December had prompted me to create a poster of an imaginary week-long festivity leading up to Christmas. The center element of the poster contains a christmas-themed mailbox displaying the week's activity. Surrounding the mailbox are candy canes covered in snow. I opted to go for the red and green to convey colors of Christmas, along with the faded blue and white to convey a vintage feel to the poster.

THE ORIGINS OF THE AVANT-GARDE

Created as part of a class project at UC San Diego. The goal of this assignment was to take a document full of information pertaining to an event called The Origins of the Avant-Garde in America and transform it into a poster that would be posted around the campus. The first poster (purple) was my final iteration for this assignment.
