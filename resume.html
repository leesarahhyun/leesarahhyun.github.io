I am a designer based in Los Angeles. I attended the University of California, San Diego, where I got my degree in Cognitive Science, specializing in Design and Interaction. While I have always been interested in design, I’ve found a new interest in storytelling through motion design and animation. Ariana Grande’s “34+35” lyric video in particular has sparked my recent obsession with lyric videos, which has lead me to explore a few lyric video concepts of my own... :D

Currently, I am freelancing as a Korean translator at Tapas Media, while looking for full-time work within the design field.

During my free time, I like to watch after effects tutorial videos on youtube and like to exercise my creativity by working on personal projects. I am hoping to teach myself how to create in 3D through Blender soon!
